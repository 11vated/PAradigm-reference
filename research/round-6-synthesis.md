# Round 6 synthesis — The Intelligence Layer: Making GSPL Unsurpassable

## One-sentence answer

**GSPL's intelligence layer is unsurpassable by construction because every reasoning step is simultaneously signed, typed, lineage-tracked, graph-structured, confidence-bearing, rollback-able, and differentiable — and no frontier model or agent framework can match all seven properties without first rebuilding the substrate underneath.**

## Round 6 in one page

Round 6 answered the Round 6 charter question: given a substrate (Rounds 1–4) and an operational foundation (Round 5), what intelligence layer makes GSPL unsurpassable versus Claude, Gemini, GPT, Qwen, DeepSeek, Llama, Mistral, xAI Grok, and every coding agent (Claude Code, Qwen Code, Gemini CLI, Cursor, Aider, Cline, Continue, Copilot Workspace, Devin, SWE-agent, OpenHands, Augment)?

The answer is not a feature list and not parity. It is a seven-axis structural claim (Brief 131):

1. **Signed** — every artifact carries cryptographic creator identity.
2. **Typed** — every artifact has a substrate type from the 11-edge ontology.
3. **Lineage-tracked** — every artifact has a signed provenance graph.
4. **Graph-structured** — every artifact lives in the federation graph.
5. **Confidence-bearing** — every artifact carries a three-part unified confidence (neural + symbolic + graph).
6. **Rollback-able** — every artifact is revertible in one operation; trajectories compose.
7. **Differentiable** — every modifier-surface action has a gradient where the type supports it.

No published system has more than three of these properties simultaneously. All seven together compound into eight capability claims:

- Forever-auditable reasoning.
- Drift-free self-improvement.
- Structural multi-agent coordination.
- Cryptographic creator ownership.
- Generation-time grounding guarantees.
- Recoverable long-horizon autonomy.
- Learning and using as the same loop.
- Native neurosymbolic composition.

## What Round 6 refused

- **AutoGen/CrewAI conversational multi-agent:** rejected. Role-play degrades grounding. GSPL uses namespace-routed MoE and bounded returning sub-agents.
- **MetaGPT waterfall:** rejected. Rigid decomposition hurts non-linear work.
- **Unrestricted Computer Use:** gated. Only as last-resort external tool with mandatory preview and sandbox.
- **Full world models (Dreamer/Genie/Sora):** deferred. Substrate IS a world model at a higher abstraction.
- **Hybrid SSM kernel (Mamba/Jamba/Titans):** deferred to v0.4. YaRN-extended attention suffices for v0.1.
- **Closed-frontier backbone dependency:** rejected. Open-weight Qwen3/Llama 4/DeepSeek are the v1 backbones; closed models are optional adapters.
- **Proprietary weights, prompts, or training data copying:** absolute refusal.
- **Unauditable reasoning:** refused by construction (every step is a signed gseed).
- **Unrollbackable self-improvement:** refused by construction (every adapter is rollback-able).
- **Unamendable constitutional commitments:** preserved (the 13 are non-amendable).
