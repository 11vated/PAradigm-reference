# ADR-NNN: Title

**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-XXX
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Deciders:** Names / handles
**Layer:** Which of the 7 layers this affects (or "Cross-cutting")

## Context

What is the problem? What forces are at play? What constraints do we have?
Be concrete about why this decision matters and why it has to be made now.

## Decision

What did we decide? State it in the active voice: "We will use X."
Be specific enough that an engineer can implement against this without further questions.

## Consequences

What becomes easier? What becomes harder? What is now true that wasn't before?
Cover both positive and negative consequences. List them honestly.

## Alternatives Considered

What other options did we look at? For each:

- **Option A** — short description, why we did not pick it
- **Option B** — short description, why we did not pick it

The alternatives section is the most important part of the ADR. Future maintainers will want to know whether their bright new idea has already been considered (and rejected) for reasons that still apply.

## References

Links to papers, RFCs, or external documentation that informed the decision.
